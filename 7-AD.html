3 Audio devices to up your Style

Are Audio Devices really a trend? Something that you should catch up on?

Audio devices like headphones, earphones, portable speakers, amps, etc are a real trend now. They are one important accessory for everyday life now. To read more about it read more on check out Wearable/Portable Audio devices in Fashion

  • THE JBL TUNE 220TWS

    This one is the that you should really go for, for it's supreme sound quality and the design. This looks very trendy and is available in colours for every choice. It comes in mocha brown-gray, a subtle blue, a quiet pink, green version which is brighter and definitely a black and a white. The DESIGN looks very chic, perfect for the college students, working millenials and is loved by the new age entrepreneurs. It looks a lot like the Apple AirPods but it is half the price and a it is a new product in the market, so not a lot of your friends will have it.

    Beats Solo3

    Beats solo is a beast of the headphones and that comes under the premium headphone segment. It has a simple design and is overall round. It is available in colours like satin silver, gold black and white. They Also came up with the new beats club collection with mixing colors that you check in the picture below. With these simple yet elegant colours, the rich love it. This overall gives a very rich look for it's design and the price it comes from. A lot of celebrities like Khalid, Travis Scott, Nicki Minaj, etc have been seen wearing this.

    JBL Partybox

    This is the absolute PARTY banger, this speaker has crazy colorful lights to it that sync in with the music. It creates the perfect vibe and attracts attention for it's size and looks. This a comparatively bigger speaker for all the crazy house parties, it is big in size and a little heave but has an attached trolley to it for functionality. This is a go to speaker if you have regular house parties and want to make them 'cooler'. This would be a delight for all the college goers.
